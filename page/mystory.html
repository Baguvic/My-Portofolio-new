My Story

About Me

Hello, I'm Bagus, and I've recently graduated from Universitas Sebelas April with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. My journey into the world of technology began during my fourth semester of college. Since then, I've been captivated by the ever-evolving world of technology, and it has become my passion. While pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, I discovered my true calling within the field: Front-End Development. There's something incredibly rewarding about taking a concept and turning it into a visually stunning, user-friendly website. I firmly believe that the front-end of a website serves as its face to the world, and I'm dedicated to creating memorable and enjoyable online experiences for users.

Frontend Developer

I found myself irresistibly drawn into the realm of Front-End Development when I first ventured into the world of Figma. As I began honing my design skills and gradually transitioned into a coder, translating my Figma designs into HTML and CSS, it felt like performing digital alchemy. The sheer satisfaction I derived from witnessing my visual creations come to life in the digital landscape was nothing short of magical. This experience laid the cornerstone of my journey towards becoming a passionate Front-End Developer.

In the projects ahead, I am determined to continue chasing creativity and innovation, delivering websites that are not just functional but also captivating. I am incredibly excited to see where this journey will take me and how my contributions to the frontend world will continue to evolve.

Thank you for this opportunity, and may we all continue to inspire and enrich the online world with every step we take.
